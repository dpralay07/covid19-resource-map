Covid Crisis Map

1. The web app will automatically point the map to your current location. 2. You can also click on the search button on the left side to point the map to any other location. Search queries can be any state, city, street etc. 3. Mouse click will create a marker. Click on the marker to enter the properties of the resource. Current properties are: Name, Type (Grocery Shop, Hospital, Gas station), Opening time, Closing time, Phone number and Remarks. 4. Click on Save Changes button to save the properties of the marker. 5. The marker will be saved on the same location.
